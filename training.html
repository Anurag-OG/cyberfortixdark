Cybersecurity Professional Certificate

Network Engineer

This course equips you with the expertise to design, implement, and secure complex enterprise networks. From configuring high-performance infrastructure to deploying advanced security measures, you'll gain the skills to keep networks resilient and efficient in the face of evolving cyber threats.

  • In-depth network architecture and protocol mastery
  • Advanced firewall, VPN, and IDS/IPS configurations
  • Hands-on troubleshooting with Wireshark and packet analysis

Ethical Hacker

Step into the mindset of an attacker to strengthen cyber defenses. This course covers penetration testing, vulnerability assessment, and ethical hacking techniques using the latest tools, empowering you to identify and fix security weaknesses before malicious actors exploit them.

  • Vulnerability assessment and penetration testing fundamentals
  • Exploitation techniques using Kali Linux and industry-standard tools
  • Reporting and remediation best practices for real-world scenarios

SOC Analyst

As a SOC Analyst, you'll be the first line of defense against cyberattacks. This course trains you in real-time threat monitoring, detection, and incident response, enabling you to act swiftly and decisively to protect an organisation's critical assets.

  • SIEM platform operations (Splunk, QRadar, Elastic)
  • Threat detection, triage, and incident response workflows
  • Log analysis, correlation rules, and threat intelligence

GRC Analyst

Gain the skills to bridge the gap between cybersecurity and business governance. This course focuses on risk management, compliance frameworks, and security policies, preparing you to safeguard organisations while ensuring regulatory adherence.

  • Understanding frameworks like ISO 27001, NIST, and GDPR
  • Compliance audits and security governance processes
  • Aligning business objectives with cybersecurity strategy
